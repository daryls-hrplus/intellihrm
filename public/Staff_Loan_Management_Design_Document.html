Staff Loan Management Module

Design Document

Executive Summary

A multi-territory staff loan management system for 8 Caribbean locations (Bahamas, Barbados, Bermuda, BVI, Cayman, Guyana, Jamaica, Trinidad) with varying loan types, disbursement mechanisms, and repayment policies.

Functional Requirements

1. Loan Configuration (Admin)

  • Define loan types per company/territory
  • Configure interest rates (fixed, variable, bank O/D rate)
  • Set maximum loan amounts by employee level
  • Define repayment periods and schedules
  • Configure eligibility rules (e.g., managers+ for car lease)

2. Loan Application & Approval

  • Employee loan request form
  • Configurable approval workflow (HC → Finance → Partner)
  • Document attachment support
  • Eligibility validation against rules

3. Disbursement Methods

  • Direct Deposit (EFT) - Primary method
  • Cheque Generation - For new hires without bank accounts
  • Petty Cash - For small amounts (Trinidad, Barbados)
  • Third-Party Payment - Car leases, medical, vendors
  • Off-Cycle Payroll - Salary advances

4. Repayment Tracking

  • Salary Deduction - Automatic payroll integration
  • Direct Deposit by Staff - Manual payment tracking
  • Over Counter - Cash payments
  • Configurable repayment schedules (monthly, per pay cycle)

5. Car Lease Management (Trinidad-specific)

  • Vehicle quotation tracking
  • Lessor invoice management
  • Benefit-in-Kind (BIK) tax calculation (25%)
  • 48-60 month lease tracking

6. Recharge Management

  • DHL charges tracking
  • Cell phone charges
  • Credit card personal expenses
  • Insurance recharges

7. Reporting

  • Outstanding loans by territory
  • Repayment schedule reports
  • Deduction lists for payroll
  • Loan aging analysis

Data Model

Primary Tables:

loan_types - Loan type configuration per company

  • id, company_id, name, code
  • has_interest, interest_rate, interest_type
  • max_repayment_months, eligibility_criteria
  • third_party_payment, start_date, end_date

staff_loans - Individual loan records

  • id, employee_id, loan_type_id, company_id
  • principal_amount, interest_rate, total_amount
  • disbursement_date, disbursement_method, status
  • approved_by, workflow_instance_id

loan_repayments - Repayment schedule and tracking

  • id, staff_loan_id, amount, due_date, paid_date
  • payment_method, status, payroll_run_id

car_leases - Car lease specific data (Trinidad)

  • id, staff_loan_id, employee_id, vehicle_details
  • lessor_vendor_id, monthly_lease_amount
  • bik_amount, tax_amount, lease_months

loan_recharges - Recharge tracking

  • id, employee_id, company_id, recharge_type
  • amount, incurred_date, status

Integration Points

  1. Payroll Module - Salary deductions, off-cycle payments
  2. Accounts Payable - Third-party payments, vendor disbursements
  3. Workflow Engine - Approval routing
  4. GL Interface - Journal entries, cost center allocation
  5. Employee Self-Service - Loan requests, balance viewing

Territory-Specific Requirements

Bahamas

  • New hire loan: $6,000, 12-month repayment
  • Relocation: $700 flat
  • Disbursement: EFT or manual cheque

Barbados

  • Salary advance: 2 per fiscal year limit
  • Includes: cell phone, credit cards, DHL, courier services
  • Nectar points cash-out tracking

Bermuda

  • New hire loan: $4,000, 8-month repayment ($500/month)
  • Relocation: $2,000 (managers), more for directors
  • Petty cash for initial $500

Cayman

  • New hire loan: $4,000-$6,000 (employee choice), 12-month repayment
  • Staff help loans: emergency funds
  • High volume: 50 new hire loans/year

Jamaica

  • Staff loans: 9.75% interest (bank O/D rate)
  • Educational loans (ACCA/CPA): 82/year, 6-month repayment
  • Medical loans: interest-free
  • Bank loan and NHA deductions via payroll

Trinidad

  • Car lease program: Managers+ only, 48-60 months
  • BIK calculation: 50% of lease (net of VAT) at 25% tax
  • Recharges: roaming, insurance, DHL, credit card
